5. Benchmark table

Across our eval set, end-to-end p95 latency by configuration:

  • Naive RAG: 3,100 ms
  • Tuned chunking + embedding cache: 1,950 ms
  • Above + NimbusVector-Turbo managed index: 920 ms

6. Conclusion and recommendation

The single highest-leverage change was migrating our vector store to the NimbusVector-Turbo managed index, which alone delivered a 2.1x end-to-end latency improvement over our tuned self-hosted setup.
